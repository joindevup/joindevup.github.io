About DevUp

DevUp is a pioneering startup methodology designed to equip developers for the challenges of the AI era — blending technical mastery, emotional intelligence, and strategic career acceleration.

Problems We Solve

  • Oversupply of junior devs with outdated or shallow training
  • Shortage of emotionally resilient and adaptable professionals
  • High rates of burnout and stagnation in tech careers

Our Solution

A complete training ecosystem powered by our Dev Hero platform. It blends AI-powered tools, self-awareness practices, and career design strategies for sustainable high-performance.

Components include: Daily Monitor, Career Debugger, MasterClasses, Expert Link, Recruiter Link, and more.

How It Works

Through our platform, users access personalized content, interactive dashboards, strategic planning tools, and one-click access to experts — all designed to empower learning and fulfillment.

Impact

Our beta cohort saw 100% career upgrades in < 6 months. Over 1,500 developers trained by our team. Grounded in PhD-level research and real-life mentorship programs.
